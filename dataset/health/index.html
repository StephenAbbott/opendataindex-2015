  • Location of public hospitals and clinics.
  • Data on infectious diseases rates in a country.

7 Canada

Canada as a federation has divisions of power, In Canada health is paid for via transfer payments from the Federal government but administered by the provinces and territories. The health data being requested in this category come from a number of sources, and some would have open licences and some would not.

Some data are free some are not.

The Public Health Agency of Canada disseminate some infections disease data via their health surveillance page http://www.phac-aspc.gc.ca/surveillance-eng.php

Statistics Canada also disseminates health data here - http://www.statcan.gc.ca/eng/health/index

A non-governmental organization, the Canadian Institute for Health Information, manages and disseminates provincial data supplied by the provinces and territories on the administration of the health case system.

https://www.cihi.ca/en/about-cihi https://www.cihi.ca/en/data-and-standards

The provinces and territories have the rest.

18 Germany

There is no health performance data incluiding geo location of public hospitals and health facilities with opening hours and infectious diseases rate, updated at least once a year. Health Datasets of the statstical office temporarily available https://www.gbe-bund.de/gbe10/hrecherche.prc_datenquellen?p_aid=67649880&p_uid=gast&p_sprache=D&p_knoten=STBA&tk=51310&tk2=51311&cnt_ut=1&ut=51311

The ' Robert Koch-Institut' (RKI) specifically the Aqua-Institut (works under the directive of the Ministry of Health) does evaluations of which some are available upon request at the institute. http://www.healthdatanavigator.eu/germany/80-data-source/132-germany-data-source and via several unofficial searchengines i.e. https://www.weisse-liste.de/de/krankenhaus/krankenhaussuche/ or other insurance companies websites. The data is existing as it is mandatory for individual hospitals to publish external quality audits. In the future the newly founded (01/2015) 'Institut für Transparenz und Qualitätssicherung im Gesundheitswesen' will be responsible: http://www.iqtig.org/index According to the department for the health monitoring (Gesunheitsmonitoring) in the RKI the topic is currently given more and more priority.
